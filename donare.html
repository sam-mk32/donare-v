✦ Our Story

Giving made transparent,
trust made permanent.

Donare was built on a simple belief — that every act of generosity deserves accountability, and every donor deserves to see the impact of their contribution.

Our Mission

Donare exists to bridge the gap between generous donors and the NGOs doing critical work on the ground. We built a centralized platform where campaigns are verified, donations are tracked transparently, and receipts are issued automatically.

Whether you're contributing ₹ 10 or ₹ 10,000 — your giving is recorded, acknowledged, and directed exactly where you intend it to go.

We partner exclusively with registered NGOs who share our commitment to accountability and measurable impact.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get my donation receipt?
Receipts are emailed automatically right after your donation is confirmed.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes — simply enter "Anonymous" as your name when donating.
Are donations tax-deductible?
Donations to registered NGOs qualify for tax exemption under Section 44(6) ITA.
How do I start a campaign?
NGOs can apply for a trustee account via hello@donare.org.
